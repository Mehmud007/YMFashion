    Product Features:

    Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (2022) Wi-Fi + Cellular tablet was launched on 18th October 2022. The tablet comes with a 12.90-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 2732x2048 pixels at a pixel density of 264 pixels per inch (ppi). Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (2022) Wi-Fi + Cellular is powered by an octa-core Apple M2 processor. It comes with 8GB of RAM. As far as the cameras are concerned, the Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (2022) Wi-Fi + Cellular on the rear packs a 12-megapixel primary camera, and a 10-megapixel camera. It sports a 12-megapixel camera on the front for selfies. Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (2022) Wi-Fi + Cellular is based on iPadOS 16 and packs 128GB of inbuilt storage. The Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (2022) Wi-Fi + Cellular measures 280.60 x 214.90 x 6.40mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 682.00 grams. It was launched in Silver and Space Grey colours. Connectivity options on the Apple iPad Pro 12.9-inch (2022) Wi-Fi + Cellular include USB Type-C and Wi-Fi. Sensors on the tablet include ambient light sensor, barometer, gyroscope, and accelerometer.
